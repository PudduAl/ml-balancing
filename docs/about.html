About the 'ML-balancing' prototype

This game prototype was developed as part of a scientific study to determine whether video game levels generated and balanced using reinforcement learning would be perceived as fair/balanced by human players. The generated levels (called 'preset' in the main menu) were playtested by human players along with a short survey they had to fill out. The survey contained questions about certain properties of the levels, such as the amount and distribution of resources placed during the generation process.

Detailed information about this work can be read in the paper

"It might be balanced, but is it actually good? An Empirical Evaluation of Game Level Balancing."
